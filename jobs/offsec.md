# Offensive Security Engineer

## About Us

Teleport is the Infrastructure Identity Company, modernizing identity, access,
and policy for infrastructure, improving engineering velocity and resiliency of
critical infrastructure against human factors and/or compromise.

We are a fast-growing, well-funded Y-Combinator company. We value our craft,
are strong supporters of work/life balance, and embrace a culture of humility,
honesty, and transparency.

## About this job

We are looking for an Offensive Security Engineer to work on Teleport’s Red Team.

You will develop a strong understanding of our cloud and corporate infrastructure and use that knowledge to find gaps in our security posture.

When you find exploitation paths, you will work with the Defense and Intelligence team to add detections to Panther, Wiz, and Jamf.

You will work with your security teammates and the Cloud team to provide feedback on our cloud architecture and detection and response logic.

## Projects you may work on

* Engage with the best red teams in the world on a regular basis to test our cloud security.
* Plan and execute your own red team style cloud operations internally.
* Discover and assist in triaging cloud security architecture and configuration flaws.
* Review cloud architecture changes where your security expertise can add value. 

## Requirements

* Strong AWS security experience
* Red team / offensive security experience
* Go or Rust experience a plus
* GCP experience a plus
